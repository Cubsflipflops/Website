Welcome to The Adventures of Cubs Flip Flops

Cub is 10 years old, autistic, and full of curiosity. He would love for you to join us on our adventures and experience the world through his eyes. From the thrill of roller coasters to fun family days out, Cub’s passion for exploring shines through everything we do.

As part of our journey, we’re also on the lookout for autism-friendly places across the UK. You’ll find our honest reviews and recommendations in the Autism Friendly: Tried and Tested section on YouTube, where we share the spots that really make a difference to families like ours.

Our goal is to help raise awareness of the needs of all neurodivergent people, and to show how even small changes can have a huge impact—making days out more enjoyable for children and adults alike.

We’d love for you to follow along with our adventures, join the conversation, and connect with us. You’ll also find us on all the main social media platforms—so come and say hello, we’d be delighted to hear from you!

Thanks for watching, we hope to see you out there!

Cub, Vic and Alan
